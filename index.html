OJT Concerns

Things Learned During OJT





Acquired new skills and learned new frameworks

In the course of my training, I managed to improve my full stack development skills. In addition, I also learned how to use React JS, which is the framework used by the company. I learned how to use hooks and managing states and props which are essential to the framework.



Learned web development workflow

I learned how to use git more efficiently. In addition, I also gained insights on managing branches and pull requests, which I think is essential when working on a team.



Improved communication skills

I think that this is one of the most important things I acquired in my training. During my training, I had tasks where I had to investigate an issue and then give a detailed report so that the developers can work on it. During those times, I had to think on what and how to communicate with the developers and give important details so that they can work on it efficiently.

Over-all Comments

Overall, I had a good experience working as an intern at CodeChum. The people there were approachable. In fact, they encouraged me to ask questions when I find something confusing, even if the said question is only for clarifications. I also think that I really learned a lot while working in the company. This includes things such as adjusting to company culture, coding standards, importance of communication and details, frameworks used, and web development flow.

I am also grateful to the company since they took the risk and accepted me even if I have little experience in regards to the position I was applying.
